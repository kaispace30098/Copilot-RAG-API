### Indicator Details

**Normalizing EL Data:**  
- Final EL points (Proficiency & Growth) are calculated relative to the statewide average.  
- Because the distribution is right-skewed, outliers (points >1.5×IQR from Q1 or Q3) are removed for a more representative mean and SD.  
- Schools must qualify for both EL Proficiency and EL Growth to earn points.

**Image Description:**  
- A circle with two clockwise arrows (Copyleft symbol) represents a continuous cycle.

**Updates for 2024-2025:**  
- EL Proficiency and Growth calculations include AltELPA results.  
- Changes to EL FAY dates (see FAY in Key Terms).

### EL Proficiency Component

**Intent:**  
Award points for EL students demonstrating proficiency on AZELLA/AltELPA.  
*Requirement:* Minimum 10 EL FAY students.

**Calculation, EL Proficiency (0–5 points):**

**Inclusion Criteria:**  
- EL FAY students with an EL need (e.g., below proficient on current or prior AZELLA/AltELPA) in grades K–12.

**EL School % Proficient Calculation:**

$$
el\_sch\_pct\_prof = 100 \left[ \frac{n\_el\_proficient}{n\_el\_tested\_cy} \right]
$$

- **Variables:**  
  - **n_el_proficient:** Number of EL FAY students scoring proficient.  
  - **n_el_tested_cy:** Number of EL FAY students tested in the current year.

**EL Proficiency Statewide Average:**

$$
\text{avg\_el\_prof\_az} = \left[ \frac{\text{sum\_el\_sch\_pct\_prof}}{\text{n\_el\_eligible\_schools}} \right]
$$

- **Variables:**  
  - **sum_el_sch_pct_prof:** Sum of schools’ EL % proficient (for those meeting the EL FAY n-count).  
  - **n_el_eligible_schools:** Number of eligible schools.

### EL Growth Component

**Intent:**  
Award points for EL students who improve their AZELLA/AltELPA performance levels.

**Component Details:**  
- Minimum 10 EL FAY students with a current AZELLA/AltELPA record in the reassessment window and a prior record (from school year 2023-2024 or before the cutoff).  
- Growth is calculated only if both test records are from the same family.  
- 1 point is awarded per proficiency level increase.

**EL Proficiency Levels:**  
- **AZELLA:** Pre-Emergent/Emergent, Basic, Intermediate, Proficient  
- **KG Placement:** Pre-Emergent/Emergent, Basic/Intermediate, Proficient  
- **AltELPA:** Emerging, Progressing, Proficient

**Calculation, EL Growth (5 points):**

**EL Growth School Average Equation:**

$$
\text{avg\_el\_growth} = \frac{n\_1\text{level\_growth} + (2 \times n\_2\text{levels\_growth}) + (3 \times n\_3\text{levels\_growth})}{n\_el\_tested}
$$

- **Variables:**  
  - **n_1level_growth:** Number of EL FAY students with one-level growth.  
  - **n_2levels_growth:** Number with two-level growth.  
  - **n_3levels_growth:** Number with three-level growth.  
  - **n_el_tested:** EL FAY students with two valid AZELLA/AltELPA records.

**EL Growth Statewide Average:**

$$
avg\_el\_growth\_az = \left[ \frac{sum\_sch\_el\_avg\_growth}{n\_el\_eligible\_schools} \right]
$$

- **Variables:**  
  - **sum_sch_el_avg_growth:** Sum of school-level EL growth averages.  
  - **n_el_eligible_schools:** Number of eligible schools (meeting EL FAY n-count).
