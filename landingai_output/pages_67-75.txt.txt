Bonus Points Overview
Possible Bonus Points by Model
K-8: Total possible bonus points = 5 (Special Education: 2, Science Proficiency: 3)
9-12: Total possible bonus points = 9 (Special Education: 2, Science Proficiency: 3, CCRI: 1, ACT Aspire: 3)
Alternative 9-12: Total possible bonus points = 9 (ACT Aspire: 3, Subgroup Grad Rate: 6)
Updates for Fiscal Year 2024-2025
Science proficiency calculations exclude RAEL students.
Students with valid 8th grade MSAA are removed from ACT Aspire participation calculations.

Points:
95% or more participation: 3 points
90%-94% participation: 1.5 points
Below 90% participation: 0 points
CCRI: Military/Post-Secondary Enrollment Bonus
Eligibility: Traditional 9-12 schools only.
Criteria:
Increase in post-secondary/military enrollment for cohort 2025 compared to cohort 2024.
85% or more of cohort 2025 enrolled/enlisted.
Points: 1 point
Subgroup 5-year Graduation Rate Bonus
Eligibility: Alternative 9-12 schools only.
Subgroups: McKinney-Vento/Homeless, Foster Care, Special Education.
Criteria: Graduation rate meets or exceeds 80% of statewide average for each subgroup.
Points: 2 points per subgroup (up to 6 points total).
